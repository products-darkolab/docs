## Overview

Safua's gamification system is designed to reward consistent effort, not just completion. Every meaningful action earns XP. Streaks reward daily habits. Badges mark real milestones. Leaderboards create healthy peer competition.

## XP (Experience Points)

| Action | XP |
|---|---|
| Complete a reading lesson | +30 XP |
| Submit a lab | +75 XP |
| Complete a lab (AI review passed) | +100 XP |
| Complete a peer code review | +50 XP |
| Submit a weekly challenge | +150 XP |
| Win a monthly competition | +500 XP |
| Complete a module | +200 XP |
| Complete a level | +500 XP |
| Socratic tutor session | +10 XP |
| Daily standup post | +20 XP |

XP is cumulative and never expires. Your total XP determines your platform rank.

## Streaks

A streak is the number of consecutive days you've completed at least one learning activity (reading a lesson, submitting a lab, or posting a standup).

### Protecting your streak

- **Focus Freeze** — earn one by completing a module. Protects your streak for one missed day. Activate from your dashboard before midnight.
- **Weekend Rest Mode** — enable in settings to exclude Saturdays and Sundays from your streak calculation.

<Warning>
  Focus Freezes don't stack. You can only hold one at a time.
</Warning>

## Badges

Badges are awarded for reaching specific milestones. They appear on your public profile.

| Badge | How to earn |
|---|---|
| 🔥 First Flame | Complete your first lesson |
| 🧪 Lab Rat | Submit your first lab |
| 📅 Week Streak | 7-day learning streak |
| 🌙 Night Owl | Complete 5 lessons between 10pm–2am |
| 🎯 Sniper | Score 95+ on 3 consecutive labs |
| 🏆 Top Dog | Finish first in a monthly competition |
| 🤝 Good Reviewer | Complete 10 peer code reviews |
| 🎓 Foundation Complete | Complete all 6 Foundation modules |
| 🚀 Track Complete | Complete a full career track |

## Leaderboards

Three leaderboard scopes:

| Leaderboard | What it shows |
|---|---|
| **Cohort** | Weekly XP ranking within your cohort — resets Monday |
| **Track** | All-time XP ranking for learners on your career track |
| **Global** | All-time XP ranking across all Safua learners |

Your rank is always visible in the sidebar.
