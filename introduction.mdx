## What is Safua?

Safua is an AI-powered learning platform built by [DarkoLab](https://darkolab.io) that combines a **living knowledge graph**, **multi-agent AI mentoring**, and **simulated company environments** to train the next generation of AI and data professionals.

Unlike traditional courses that go stale, Safua's curriculum is connected to live industry signals — job postings, GitHub trends, and arXiv research — ensuring every skill you learn is relevant to what employers need today.

## The DARKO Method

Safua is built around the DARKO learning method — a 4-stage framework that mirrors how real AI engineers work:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Discover">
    Understand the concept through guided reading and real-world context.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply">
    Build it yourself in a Monaco-powered lab with AI code review.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reflect">
    Spaced repetition reviews lock in your learning before it decays.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Koach">
    Your AI mentor and cohort peers push your understanding deeper through Socratic dialogue.
  </Step>
</Steps>
