## Overview

The Data Scientist track trains you to extract insight from data and build models that drive decisions. You'll move from exploratory analysis, through statistical modelling and A/B testing, to deploying and monitoring models in production.

**Prerequisites:** Foundation track completed
**Duration:** ~20 weeks | **Labs:** 14 | **Capstone:** Kaggle-style competition

## Curriculum

### Level 1 — Exploratory Data Analysis (~4 weeks)
EDA fundamentals, Pandas advanced, Matplotlib/Seaborn, storytelling with data, feature distributions, outlier detection.

### Level 2 — Statistical Modelling (~4 weeks)
Linear/logistic regression, regularisation, Bayesian modelling, time series forecasting, causal inference basics.

### Level 3 — Experimentation (~4 weeks)
A/B testing, statistical power, multiple comparisons, Bayesian A/B, experiment design, bandit algorithms.

### Level 4 — ML in Production (~5 weeks)
Feature engineering, gradient boosting (XGBoost/LightGBM), model evaluation, class imbalance, ML pipelines with scikit-learn, model explanation (SHAP).

### Level 5 — Capstone (~3 weeks)
Full Kaggle-style competition: EDA → feature engineering → modelling → evaluation report. Highest AUC wins **+500 XP**.
