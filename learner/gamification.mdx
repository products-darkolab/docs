## XP (Experience Points)

Everything you do on Safua earns XP:

| Action | XP |
|---|---|
| Complete a lesson | 10 XP |
| Submit a lab | 50 XP |
| Complete a weekly challenge | 150 XP |
| Complete a peer review | 50 XP |
| Win the monthly competition | 500 XP |
| Maintain a 7-day streak | Bonus multiplier |
| Level up in league | 100 XP bonus |

## Streaks

Come back every day to build your streak. Your streak multiplies your XP earnings.

### Focus Freezes
Miss a day? Use a **Focus Freeze** to protect your streak. You get a free Focus Freeze when you sign up, and earn more by completing modules.

### Streak Wagers
Feeling confident? Wager **50 XP** on a 7-day streak target. If you hit it, you win **100 XP** (2x return). If you break the streak, you lose the 50 XP. Set a wager from your streak panel on the dashboard.

### Weekend Rest Mode
Enable **Weekend Rest Mode** in settings to exclude weekends from your streak calculation. Ideal if you're using Safua as a weekday learning habit.

## Leagues

Every week, you compete in a league of ~30 learners. Your league tier determines who you compete against:

```
Diamond
   ↕
Platinum
   ↕
Gold
   ↕
Silver
   ↕
Bronze  ← everyone starts here
```

At the end of each week:
- **Top 10** in your group promote to the next tier
- **Bottom 5** demote to the tier below
- Everyone else stays

Your league position is visible on the `/leagues` page with a weekly XP leaderboard and your promotion/demotion zone.

## Spaced Repetition

The platform automatically schedules **review sessions** for skills you've learned, using the SM-2 algorithm. You'll see a "Skills to Review" notification when concepts are about to decay. A 5-minute review session resets the decay timer and re-earns mastery.

Reviews are short: 3–5 questions per skill, adaptive to your last performance.
