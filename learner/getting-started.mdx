## Your First Week

<Steps>
  <Step title="Day 1: Choose your track">
    After registering, you'll be prompted to choose a career track. Don't overthink this — you're choosing a direction, not a life sentence. The Foundation curriculum is the same for everyone.

    If you're unsure: pick **AI Engineer** if you want to build products, **Data Scientist** if you want to run experiments and build models, **ML Engineer** if you want to train models at scale, or **Data Engineer** if you want to build infrastructure.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Days 1–3: Start Foundation">
    Open your first module: Python Fundamentals. Read through the lessons at your own pace. When you hit a lab, write the code in the Monaco editor and submit it — the AI code review will give you feedback within seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Day 4: Meet your AI tutor">
    Click the chat bubble on any lesson to open the AI tutor. Try asking it to explain something from the lesson. If you want a real challenge, toggle **Socratic mode** — it will never give you the answer, but will guide you to find it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Day 5+: Build your streak">
    Come back every day. Even 15 minutes counts. Your streak builds XP multipliers and earns you league points. Miss a day and you lose the streak — unless you use a Focus Freeze.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Understanding Your Dashboard

| Widget | What it shows |
|---|---|
| Track Progress | Your career track + Foundation completion |
| Skill Radar | Your mastery across the top skills in your track |
| Streak Counter | Current streak, XP, and league position |
| ZPD Recommendations | Top skills the system thinks you're ready to learn next |
| Cohort Panel | Your cohort's weekly challenge and leaderboard |

## Tips for Learning Effectively

- **Don't skip the labs.** Reading is passive. The lab is where learning happens.
- **Read the AI code review carefully.** The inline comments in the Monaco editor are your most valuable feedback.
- **Use Socratic mode when you're stuck.** Arriving at the answer yourself makes it stick 10x longer.
- **Post a standup.** Even a single sentence about what you learned keeps your cohort alive and your streak active.
- **Do the weekly challenge.** It's designed to stretch you just beyond your current level — that's the zone where growth happens.
