## What Makes the Safua Tutor Different

Most AI tutors just answer your question. The Safua tutor asks you one back.

It's built on the **Socratic method** — a teaching approach where the teacher guides you to discover the answer yourself through a sequence of questions. Research consistently shows that active recall (arriving at the answer yourself) produces stronger, more durable learning than passive explanation.

The tutor **never reveals the answer directly**. If you're stuck after three exchanges, it gives you a hint — but even the hint is framed as a question.

## Socratic Mode

Every lesson has a chat bubble in the bottom right. By default, the tutor is in **Standard mode** — it explains concepts clearly and directly. Toggle **Socratic mode** to switch to pure Socratic dialogue.

| | Standard Mode | Socratic Mode |
|---|---|---|
| Best for | First reading of new concepts | Checking your understanding |
| Style | Direct explanation | Guiding questions |
| When stuck | Explains the answer | Gives a hint question |
| XP bonus | — | +10 XP per session |

## What the Tutor Knows About You

The tutor isn't starting from scratch in every session. It has memory of your past interactions:

- Skills you've struggled with ("async/await mental model")
- Skills you're strong in ("pandas groupby operations")
- Your preferred explanation style
- Questions you've asked before

This means it calibrates its questions and explanations to your actual level — not a generic learner.

## Code Review in Labs

When you submit a lab, the **Code Review Agent** gives you structured feedback:

- **Overall score** out of 100
- **Correctness** — does it produce the right output?
- **Style** — is it readable, well-named, Pythonic?
- **Efficiency** — is there a better algorithm or approach?
- **Inline comments** — appear directly in the Monaco editor

In the Monaco editor, inline comments appear as coloured glyphs in the margin:
- 🔴 **Red** — hint: something needs fixing here
- 🔵 **Blue** — Socratic question: think about what this is doing
- 🟢 **Green** — praise: this is well done

Click any glyph to read the full comment.

## Motivation Nudges

The **Motivation Agent** sends in-app notifications when:

- You haven't been active for 48 hours
- Your streak is at risk (expiring in < 4 hours)
- You're within 10 XP of moving up in your league
- A skill you've learned has decayed below 50% mastery

These are delivered as push notifications in the TopBar bell icon. They're designed to be useful, not spammy — at most one per day.

## Tips for Getting the Most Out of the Tutor

- **Answer the question before asking another one.** The tutor builds on your responses.
- **Say "I don't know" explicitly.** It will give you a hint rather than staying stuck.
- **Use it to rubber-duck.** Start with "Let me try to explain this back to you..." — the tutor will correct you if you're wrong.
- **Ask "why" not just "how."** The tutor is better at helping you understand than giving you code to copy.
